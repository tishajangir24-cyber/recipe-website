Mom's Momos

More delicious than street one's!

⏱️ Prep: 90 min 🔥 Cook: 60 min ⏳ Total: 150 min 👥 Serves: 5 📊 Medium 🥦 Vegetarian

🛒 Ingredients

  • Dough Ingredients :
  • All-purpose flour (maida) - 1 to 2 cups
  • Salt - ½ to 1 tsp
  • Oil - 1 tsp
  • Water - ¾ to 1 cup (lukewarm or cold)
  • Vegetable Filling Ingredients :
  • Cabbage - ½ to 1 cup, finely chopped
  • Carrot - ½ cup, finely grated
  • Onion - ½ cup, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 2-3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 tsp, minced
  • Green chilies - 1-2, chopped (optional)
  • Black pepper powder - ½ tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Special ingredient :
  • Mom's love

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the Dough:Mix 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tsp oil, and salt in a bowl. Gradually add lukewarm water and knead into a smooth, firm dough. Cover with a damp cloth and rest for 30 minutes.
2
Make the Filling:Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Sauté minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies for 1 minute. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent (3 mins). Add carrots and cabbage, sauté for 8–10 minutes. Season with soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Cool completely.
3
Shape the Momos:Divide dough into small lemon-sized balls. Roll each into a 3–4 inch disc, keeping the center thicker than edges. Place 1 tsp filling in the center. Pleat the edges starting from one side, bringing them together to form a sealed pouch (half-moon or round shape).
4
Steam the Momos:Grease a steamer tray or line with cabbage leaves. Arrange momos with space between them. Steam over boiling water for 10–12 minutes until dough turns slightly translucent and shiny.
5
Serve Hot:Serve immediately with spicy red chutney, green chutney, or tomato sauce.
