Idli Cake

The most yummy and newly innovated by only and only my mom!.

⏱️ Prep: 20 min 🔥 Cook: 10 min ⏳ Total: 30 min 👥 Serves: 4 📊 Medium 🥦 Vegetarian

🛒 Ingredients

  • Suji
  • Curd
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Special ingredient :
  • Mom's love

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare Batter:In a bowl, mix 2 cups suji (semolina), 1 cup curd (yogurt), 1 tsp salt, 1 tablespoon of baking soda, and ¾ cup water. Stir well.
2
Grease Molds:Lightly grease idli molds with oil or ghee to prevent sticking.
3
Pour:Pour it in cake container.
4
Steam Idli Cake:Pour batter into mold. Steam in a preheated Cake maker for 10-12 minutes on medium heat.
5
Check Doneness:Insert a toothpick or knife. If it comes out clean, idlis are done.
6
Cool & Serve:Let cool for 2 minutes, then gently remove with a spoon. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
