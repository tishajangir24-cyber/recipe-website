Bread Pizza

Mom's slow-cooked treasure — spicy, cheesy, and impossibly tasty.

⏱️ Prep: 20 min 🔥 Cook: 30 min ⏳ Total: 50 min 👥 Serves: 4 📊 Easy 🥦 Vegetarian

🛒 Ingredients

  • Bread slices (white, whole wheat, or any sandwich bread)
  • Pizza sauce or tomato ketchup (homemade or store-bought)
  • Cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, or pizza cheese, grated)
  • Vegetable toppings (onion, capsicum/bell pepper, tomato, olives, sweet corn)
  • Oil or butter (for toasting the bread)
  • Herbs and seasonings (oregano, chili flakes, black pepper)
  • Suji-curd mixture (batter)
  • Special ingredient :
  • Mom's love

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare toppings:Chop onions, tomatoes, capsicum, and mix with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
2
Toast bread (optional):Lightly toast one side of bread on a tawa for crispiness.
3
Assemble:Spread pizza sauce or ketchup on the bread, add vegetables, top with generous mozzarella cheese and put suji-curd mixture.
4
Cook on tawa:Heat a non-stick tawa on low flame, add butter, place assembled bread slice cheese-side up, and cover with a lid.
5
Melt cheese:Cook for 2-4 minutes until cheese melts and bread base turns golden.
6
Serve hot:Remove, garnish with oregano or chili flakes, and enjoy.
Use a lid to trap heat and melt the cheese evenly without drying the bread.
