Cake

Soft, pillowy, choco-scented — Mom's dessert that turns every birthday special.

⏱️ Prep: 60 min 🔥 Cook: 30 min ⏳ Total: 90 min 👥 Makes: 8 pieces 📊 Medium 🍮 Dessert

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 1¾ to 2 cups (220-270g) all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup (64-85g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 to 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ to ¾ teaspoon salt
  • For liquid :
  • 1 to 1½ cups warm water or hot coffee
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) oil
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Special ingredient :
  • Mom's love

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)Grease and line two 9-inch round cake pans with parchment paper.
2
Mix dry ingredients:In a large bowl, whisk together 2 cups sugar, 1¾ cups flour, ¾ cup cocoa powder, 1½ tsp baking soda, 1½ tsp baking powder, and ½ tsp salt.
3
Combine wet ingredients:In another bowl, beat 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, ½ cup oil, 2 tsp vanilla, and 1 cup warm coffee or water until smooth.
4
Make batter:Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Mix until combined. Add boiling water last—the batter will be thin.
5
Bake:Divide batter evenly into pans. Bake 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
6
Cool:Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes. Then remove, peel off parchment, and cool completely on a wire rack.
7
Frost:Once cooled, frost with chocolate buttercream or ganache. Assemble layers and decorate as desired.s
